# VIVERSE Official Portals

This document lists the official and verified URLs for various VIVERSE portals and tools.

## Creation & Management
- **VIVERSE Studio**: [https://studio.viverse.com/](https://studio.viverse.com/)
  - Purpose: The primary dashboard for creators to manage, track, and optimize their Worlds and other creations in one place.
- **VIVERSE Create**: [https://create.viverse.com/](https://create.viverse.com/)
  - Purpose: The main entry point for world creation and accessing the suite of VIVERSE creation tools.

## Identity & Social
- **VIVERSE Avatar**: [https://avatar.viverse.com/](https://avatar.viverse.com/)
  - Purpose: Create, customize, and manage your VIVERSE avatars.

## Exploration
- **VIVERSE Explore (Worlds)**: [https://worlds.viverse.com/](https://worlds.viverse.com/)
  - Purpose: Browse, search, and enter existing VIVERSE worlds.
- **World URL Pattern**: `https://worlds.viverse.com/[hub_sid]`
  - Note: Use the `hub_sid` property for the most reliable clickable links.
